## Context

We have a client, **AX3**, that buys and sells music intellectual property (IP), including **physical** and **digital** music products.

AX3’s current system has a core limitation:

* A **physical product must always be created first**
* **Digital-only products are not first-class entities**

### Main objectives

1. Enable **digital-only products** as first-class entities
2. **Accelerate and automate** the workflow for creating:

   * physical + digital products
   * digital-only products

### Secondary objective

* Enable selling music **only on ax3.com** (and not on Legato), when desired.

A deep-dive meeting was conducted with AX3. Notes and documentation are attached.
